# Sales Performance Visualization Dashboard (Power BI)

Interactive Power BI dashboard built on the Sample Superstore dataset to analyze sales, profit and customer performance across regions, segments and products.

## Project Overview
- Tracks key KPIs such as Total Sales, Total Orders, Profit Margin %, and Total Profit.
- Provides drill-down views for regions, product categories, sub-categories and customers.
- Designed as a three-page report for executives, managers and sales reps.

## Pages

### 1. Executive Summary
- KPI cards for Total Sales, Total Orders, Profit Margin %, Total Profit.
- Line chart showing Sales vs Sales Last Year by Year.
- Bar chart for Total Sales by Region and donut chart for Total Sales by Segment.

### 2. Regional & Product Analysis
- Matrix view by Region → Category → Sub-Category with Total Sales, Total Profit and Profit Margin %.
- Top N products chart based on Total Sales.
- Profit by Region chart with date and region slicers.

### 3. Customer & Rep Detail
- Customer table with Customer Name, Segment, Region, Total Sales, Total Profit and Total Orders.
- Top 10 Customers by Sales bar chart.
- Filters for Date, Region and Segment for focused analysis.

## Tech Stack
- Power BI Desktop
- DAX measures: Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Orders, Profit Margin %, Sales LY, Sales YoY %.
